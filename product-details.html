Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our blister-proof socks, fit, and shipping.

A foot blister is formed from improper fitting shoes rubbing against the skin. The repetitive rubbing can cause irritation and create a painful friction blister.

Shezza creates never before done foam-padded socks that eliminate the pain of breaking in new shoes. Our anti blister socks have a cloud-soft foam patch sewn into the back of the heel to protect your foot from ever getting a blister again. The patch acts as a strong barrier between the friction of your shoe and your foot, so you will never get a blister again!
